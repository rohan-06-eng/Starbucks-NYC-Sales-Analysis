# Project Name: Starbucks Coffee Dashboard: NYC Sales Analysis

## Description
This project aims to analyze Starbucks' sales performance across various locations in New York City, with a focus on month-on-month (MoM) sales growth, order patterns, and product quantities sold. The dashboard provides actionable insights into store-level performance and sales dynamics using visualizations and KPIs.

## KPI's Requirements
1. **Total Sales Analysis:**
   - Calculate the total sales for each month to track revenue generation.
   - Determine the month-on-month (MoM) increase or decrease in sales to understand trends.
   - Highlight the difference in sales between the selected month and the previous month to identify any performance shifts.

2. **Total Orders Analysis:**
   - Calculate the total number of orders for each month to monitor customer engagement.
   - Determine the MoM increase or decrease in the number of orders to assess the changes in customer activity.
   - Highlight the difference in the number of orders between the selected month and the previous month to track order patterns.

3. **Total Quantity Sold Analysis:**
   - Calculate the total quantity of items sold each month to gauge product demand.
   - Determine the MoM changes in the total quantity sold to analyze sales volume trends.
   - Identify the difference in the total quantity sold between the selected month and the previous month for deeper insights.

## Charts Requirements
1. **Calendar Heat Map:**
   - A dynamic calendar heat map that adjusts based on the selected month from a slicer.
   - Each day is color-coded to reflect sales volume, with darker shades representing higher sales.
   - Implement tooltips that provide detailed metrics (Sales, Orders, Quantity) when hovering over any specific day, offering an in-depth look at daily performance.

2. **Sales Analysis by Weekdays and Weekends:**
   - Segment sales data to differentiate performance between weekdays and weekends.
   - Analyze whether sales patterns differ significantly, providing insights into customer behavior across the week.

3. **Sales Analysis by Store Location:**
   - Visualize sales data across different Starbucks store locations.
   - Incorporate MoM difference metrics to show the sales increase or decrease for each store location based on the selected month.
   - Highlight trends across locations, allowing for identification of high-performing or underperforming stores.

## Use Cases
- **Performance Tracking:** Managers can monitor store sales performance, including location-based insights, and track changes in customer behavior across time periods.
- **Customer Behavior Analysis:** By differentiating sales between weekdays, weekends, and locations, the dashboard provides a view into customer preferences.
- **Operational Decisions:** The data helps optimize inventory management and staff allocation by understanding sales peaks and dips.

## Technology Stack
- **Data Visualization:** Power BI (or similar BI tools).
- **Backend:** SQL Database storing sales, orders, and store location information.
- **Frontend:** Interactive Power BI dashboard, enabling real-time data exploration with slicers and filters.

## How to Use
- **Setup:** Ensure the backend is populated with the latest sales, orders, and product data.
- **Filter:** Use slicers to filter by month or store location for a detailed view of specific data.
- **Analyze:** Examine charts and KPIs for key insights into sales performance and order trends.
- **Export:** Export visualizations or the entire dashboard for reporting purposes.

## Conclusion
This Starbucks Coffee Dashboard is a vital tool for understanding store-level sales patterns, customer activity, and inventory demand. It empowers business leaders to make informed decisions based on clear, actionable insights, driving operational efficiency and sales growth.
